BOMBERMAN


bomberman

Instructions/Rules:

1. Win

Kill all the ghosts, find the secret door
and go inside to win.

2. Lose

When your bomberman dies (ghost kills you or
bomb kills you), you lose.

What Do You Do In This Game?

You need to kill all the ghosts (use the bomb)
and break the stones to find the secret door.
But you can't break the stones that can't break!
Hope you enjoy this game!
